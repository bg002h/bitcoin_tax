## New findings

**NEW-1. [Important] Task 15 corner-(b) / SPEC §6(b) — the fixture is staged on a path where the corner is UNREACHABLE; as written the KAT cannot pass, and the SPEC's mechanism clause repeats the error.**

The corner-(b) test stages `config_hifo()` with a method-order sale, and its comment (and SPEC §6's "(b) … re-homes a documented fee-sat basis onto the last disposal leg, **which under HIFO is the $0 tranche fragment**") asserts the documented carry lands on the tranche leg *under HIFO*. Against the engine, that composition is impossible: the fee is consumed **after** the principal from the same pool (fold.rs:636-654), and HIFO's total order consumes every `>$0` lot before any `$0` lot (`hifo_cmp` pools.rs:275-287; `consume_ordered` walks the full order, pools.rs:179-203). So whenever a HIFO method-order principal reaches the tranche (making the tranche leg last), **no documented lot remains in the pool** — the fee draw yields only `$0`-basis tranche sats, `FeeCarry.gain_basis = 0`, the re-home is a basis no-op. `tranche_leg.cost_basis > Usd::ZERO` can never go green on that staging, and Step 2 ("expect PASS") gives no sanctioned outcome — the live risk is the assertion getting weakened, unpinning the corner.

The corner itself is **real and product-reachable** — r1 I-1(b) stands in substance, but its example conflated the HIFO-last-leg fact with a fee-from-documented draw, and the fold inherited that conflation. Two live stagings:
- **(i) FIFO in-force, exact exhaustion:** pool `[D_old (documented, acquired before window_end), T (tranche), D_new (documented, acquired after window_end)]`; sale principal = `D_old + T` exactly → last leg = tranche; fee then draws FIFO from the remaining `D_new` → documented carry > 0 re-homed onto the tranche leg.
- **(ii) Specific-ID:** a named-lot sale picking the tranche (last) while a documented lot remains — `consume_principal` honors `ctx.selections` (fold.rs:63); resolve.rs states the mechanism verbatim: "on-chain `fee_sat` is excluded and consumes FIFO from the **remainder**" (resolve.rs:1122, :1172). `fee_sat` reaches `Op::Dispose` through the ReclassifyOutflow passthrough (resolve.rs:316-351).

**Fix (small, three sites):** (1) restage the corner-(b) KAT on (i) or (ii) — (ii) simplest — and drop the `config_hifo()`/HIFO comment; (2) amend SPEC §6(b)'s clause "which under HIFO is the $0 tranche fragment" to the reachable characterization (tranche leg last under the in-force order or a named-lot selection *while a documented lot remains for the FIFO fee draw*); (3) adjust Task 15's Step-2 expectation. Authority: fold.rs/pools.rs as cited; §1011 (the carry is real documented basis).
